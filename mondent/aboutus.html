About Us

Welcome to our page, where we are thrilled to introduce you to Dr. Luciano Barboza and Mondent Dental Practice. Dr. Barboza is a highly skilled dentist with expertise in Periodontology and Dental Implants. He graduated from Positivo University in Curitiba, Brazil, and has since been providing exceptional dental care to his patients.

Dr. Barboza's passion for dentistry started at a young age, and he has dedicated his life to providing quality care to his patients. With years of experience in the field, he has developed a reputation for his gentle approach and attention to detail. Dr. Barboza believes in providing personalized care to each of his patients, ensuring that they receive the best possible treatment for their unique needs.

While Dr. Barboza specializes in Periodontology and Dental Implants, he is also a skilled generalist who provides a full range of dental services. His commitment to staying up-to-date with the latest advancements in dental technology and techniques allows him to provide the most effective and efficient care possible.

At Mondent dental practice, we strive to create a comfortable and welcoming environment for our patients. Our goal is to ensure that you receive the highest quality care in a relaxed and stress-free setting. We understand that visiting the dentist can be intimidating, and we work hard to make your experience as pleasant as possible.

Thank you for taking the time to learn more about us. We look forward to meeting you and helping you achieve the healthy and beautiful smile you deserve.
